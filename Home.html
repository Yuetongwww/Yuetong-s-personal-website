About my growth environment 

I have two Eurasian Cousins. They are Lisa and Linda. We grew up together. When I visit their homes, I can hear the humorous mix of English, Mandarin and Dutch sentences. They took me to celebrate Christmas and made wishes to Santa Claus from Netherland. They would warmly introduce me to their friends and ask me to read them storybooks full of Chinese. When I was growing up, I experienced the collision and fusion of different cultures, so I am very receptive to cultural diversity.
